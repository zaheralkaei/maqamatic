# Jins Athar Kurd

## Overview

Jins Athar Kurd is a 5-note jins. It is the first jins in Maqam Athar Kurd.

---

## Interval Structure

- **Total span**: Perfect 5th (700¢)
- **Pattern**: m2 - M2 - A2 - m2
- **Character**: Dark, exotic, with augmented second creating tension

## Distinctive Features

- Begins with a minor second (Kurd-like opening)
- Contains an **augmented second** (E♭ to F#) giving it a Hijaz-like quality in the middle
- Combines Kurd and Hijaz characteristics

## Usage

- **Root jins** of Maqam Athar Kurd
- Creates a dark, mysterious mood with exotic tension
