# Jins Lami

## Overview

Jins Lami is a 4-note jins. It is the first jins in Maqam Lami.

## Interval Structure

- **Total span**: Perfect 4th (500¢) to ghammaz
- **Pattern**: m2 - M2 - M2 (100¢ - 200¢ - 200¢)
- **Character**: Somber, lamenting

## Comparison with Jins Kurd

| Jins | Basic Scale | Baggage | Character |
|------|-------------|---------|-----------|
| Kurd | D E♭ F G | C, A, B♭ | Phrygian, somber |
| Lami | D E♭ F G | A♭, B♭ | Deeper lament, darker |

The basic scale is identical to Jins Kurd. The key difference is Lami has A♭ (not A natural) in the baggage, which adds a darker, more lamenting quality when the melody extends above the ghammaz.

## Usage
- **Root jins** of Maqam Lami
- Expresses deep sadness, lamentation
- The A♭ above the ghammaz intensifies the sorrowful character
