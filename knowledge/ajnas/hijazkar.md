# Jins Hijazkar

## Overview

Jins Hijazkar has no ghammaz, and therefore no defined size. Its melody is centered around its tonic. This jins occurs on the octave of Maqam Hijazkar.

## Interval Structure

- **No ghammaz**: Melody centers around the tonic
- **No defined size**: Due to lack of ghammaz
- **Pattern**: Features two augmented seconds (A♭-B and D♭-E)
- **Character**: Highly dramatic, double-Hijaz quality

## Distinctive Features

- Contains **two augmented seconds**, giving it an intensely exotic character
- The tonic (C) is approached by a minor second from below (B-C)
- Symmetrical tension on both sides of the tonic
- Melody gravitates strongly to the tonic rather than moving toward a ghammaz

## Usage

- Occurs on the **octave of Maqam Hijazkar**
- Creates an intensely dramatic, ornate sound
- The double augmented second structure makes it one of the most exotic-sounding ajnas
