# Jins Saba Dalanshin

## Overview

Jins Saba Dalanshin is a special case of Jins Saba which has no ghammaz, and therefore no defined size. Its melody is centered around its tonic. The intervals above the tonic are more or less identical to Jins Hijaz.

## Interval Structure

- **No ghammaz**: Melody centers around the tonic
- **No defined size**: Due to lack of ghammaz
- **Pattern above tonic**: m2 - A2 - m2 (identical to Hijaz)
- **Character**: Combines Saba's approach with Hijaz-like upper structure

## Distinctive Features

- The **secondary tonic** on A (2 degrees below the main tonic) provides an additional point of melodic gravity
- Above the tonic, the intervals (m2 - A2 - m2) mirror Jins Hijaz
- The approach to the tonic uses neutral seconds (characteristic of Rast/Saba)
- Creates a unique blend of Saba and Hijaz qualities
- G in the baggage provides additional lower extension

## Comparison with Jins Hijaz

| Jins | Pattern from tonic | Notes from tonic |
|------|-------------------|------------------|
| Hijaz | m2 - A2 - m2 | D E♭ F# G |
| Saba Dalanshin | m2 - A2 - m2 | C D♭ E F |

The upper structure is identical, but Saba Dalanshin has the distinctive secondary tonic and neutral second approach below.

## Usage

- Occurs on the **octave of Maqam Rast** (which then becomes known as **Maqam Dalanshin**)
- Occurs on the **5th degree of Maqam Shawq Afza**
- Provides dramatic Hijaz-like color with the grounding of a secondary tonic
