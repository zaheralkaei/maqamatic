# Jins Nahawand

## Overview

Jins Nahawand is a 5-note jins. It is the first jins in Maqam Nahawand. It has a character similar to the Western natural minor scale.

---

## Scale (on C)

```
(Baggage)  Tonic                          Ghammaz   (Baggage)
    ↓        ↓                               ↓          ↓
   (B) ----- C -------- D -------- E♭ ------- F -------- G ----- (A♭)
        m2        M2        m2         M2         M2        m2
       (100¢)   (200¢)    (100¢)     (200¢)     (200¢)    (100¢)
```

## Interval Structure

- **Total span**: Perfect 5th (700¢) from tonic to ghammaz
- **Pattern**: M2 - m2 - M2 - M2 (200¢ - 100¢ - 200¢ - 200¢)
- **Character**: Minor quality, similar to Western natural minor

## Distinctive Features

- The minor third (E♭) gives it its characteristic minor quality
- Very similar to the first 5 notes of the Western natural minor scale
- The leading tone (B natural) in the baggage provides strong resolution to the tonic
- The A♭ baggage above the ghammaz adds darker coloring

## Usage

- **Root jins** of Maqam Nahawand
- Expresses gentle sadness, beauty with melancholy
- Familiar to Western ears due to similarity to minor mode
