# Jins Hijaz Murassa'

## Overview

Jins Hijaz Murassa' is a 4-note jins. It is not part of any maqam scale and usually occurs as a modulation from Jins Hijaz, most commonly in Maqam Hijazkar.

---

## Scale (on D)

```
(Baggage)     Tonic                 Ghammaz      (Baggage)
    ↓           ↓                      ↓             ↓
  (C#) ------- D -------- E♭ ------- F# ------- G ------- (A♭)
          m2        m2        A2         m2         m2
         (100¢)   (100¢)    (300¢)     (100¢)     (100¢)
```

## Interval Structure

- **Total span**: Perfect 4th (500¢) to ghammaz
- **Pattern**: m2 - A2 - m2 (100¢ - 300¢ - 100¢)
- **Character**: Hijaz with embellished baggage notes

## Comparison with Jins Hijaz

| Jins | Pattern | Basic Notes | Baggage |
|------|---------|-------------|---------|
| Hijaz | m2 - A2 - m2 | D E♭ F# G | B𝄳, C, A, B♭ |
| Hijaz Murassa' | m2 - A2 - m2 | D E♭ F# G | C#, A♭ |

Note: The basic scale is the same as Jins Hijaz. The difference is in the baggage notes which give Hijaz Murassa' its "embellished" (murassa') character.

## Usage

- **Not a root jins**: Does not start any maqam
- **Modulation target**: Usually occurs as a modulation from Jins Hijaz
- **Common context**: Most frequently appears in Maqam Hijazkar
