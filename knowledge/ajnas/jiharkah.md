# Jins Jiharkah

## Overview

Jins Jiharkah is a 5-note jins. It is the first jins in Maqam Jiharkah.

**Performance Note**: The 3rd and 4th degrees are usually played lower than notated.

---

## Scale (on F)

```
(Baggage)           Tonic                          Ghammaz
    ↓                 ↓                               ↓
  (D) -- (E𝄳) ----- F -------- G -------- A -------- B♭ ------- C
                          M2        M2         m2         M2
                         (200¢)    (200¢)     (100¢)     (200¢)
```

Note: E𝄳 represents E half-flat. A and B♭ are often played slightly lower in practice.

## Interval Structure

- **Total span**: Perfect 5th (700¢)
- **Pattern**: M2 - M2 - m2 - M2 (200¢ - 200¢ - 100¢ - 200¢)
- **Character**: Major quality, similar to 'Ajam but with microtonal inflections

## Distinctive Features

- Same interval structure as Jins 'Ajam
- Distinguished by the tendency to lower the 3rd and 4th degrees in performance
- This microtonal lowering gives Jiharkah its distinct flavor compared to pure 'Ajam
- The baggage includes E𝄳 (E half-flat) which connects to Rast-family sounds

## Usage
- **Root jins** of Maqam Jiharkah
- Creates a bright, major sound with subtle Arabic character from the lowered notes
