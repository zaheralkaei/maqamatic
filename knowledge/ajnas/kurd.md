## Interval Structure

- **Total span**: Perfect 4th (500¢)
- **Pattern**: m2 - M2 - M2 (100¢ - 200¢ - 200¢)
- **Character**: Phrygian quality, somber, introspective

## Distinctive Features

- Begins with a minor second, similar to the Western Phrygian mode
- The half-step at the bottom gives it a dark, grounded quality
- Simpler and more stable than Hijaz (no augmented second)
