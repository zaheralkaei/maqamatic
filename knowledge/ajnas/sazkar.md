# Jins Sazkar

## Overview

Jins Sazkar is a 5-note jins. It is the first jins in Maqam Sazkar.

---

## Scale (on C)

```
(Baggage)  Tonic                          Ghammaz   (Baggage)
    ↓        ↓                               ↓          ↓
  (B𝄳) ----- C ------- D# ------- E𝄳 ------ F -------- G ------- (A)
        N2       A2        d2         m2         M2         M2
       (150¢)  (300¢)    (50¢)      (100¢)     (200¢)     (200¢)
```

Note: B𝄳 and E𝄳 represent B half-flat and E half-flat

## Interval Structure

- **Total span**: Perfect 5th (700¢) from tonic to ghammaz
- **Pattern**: A2 - d2 - m2 - M2 (300¢ - 50¢ - 100¢ - 200¢)
- **Character**: Unusual, exotic, with augmented second and quarter-tone

## Distinctive Features

- Begins with an **augmented second** (C to D#)
- Contains a very small interval (**diminished second** / quarter-tone) between D# and E𝄳
- The D# to E𝄳 movement (50¢) is one of the smallest intervals used
- Combines the exotic augmented second with the neutral character of E half-flat

## Usage

- **Root jins** of Maqam Sazkar
- Creates an unusual, highly distinctive sound
- The quarter-tone movement (D# to E𝄳) gives it a unique ornamental quality
