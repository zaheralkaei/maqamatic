# Jins Hijaz

## Overview

Jins Hijaz is a 4-note jins. It is one of the most recognizable and dramatic ajnas in Arabic music, characterized by its augmented second interval.

---

## Scale (on D)

```
(Baggage)        Tonic                 Ghammaz         (Baggage)
    ↓              ↓                      ↓                ↓
  (B𝄳) -- (C) -- D -------- E♭ ------- F# ------- G -- (A) -- (B♭)
                      m2        A2         m2
                     (100¢)    (300¢)     (100¢)
```

Note: B𝄳 represents B half-flat

## Interval Structure

- **Total span**: Perfect 4th (500¢)
- **Pattern**: m2 - A2 - m2 (100¢ - 300¢ - 100¢)
- **Character**: Dramatic, exotic, intense, passionate

## Distinctive Features

- Contains the characteristic **augmented second** (E♭ to F#, 300¢ / 6 quarter-tones)
- The augmented second gives Hijaz its instantly recognizable "exotic" or "Middle Eastern" sound
- Framed by two minor seconds on either side

## Usage

- **Root jins** of Maqam Hijaz
- **Root jins** of Maqam Hijazkar
- **Root jins** of Maqam Zanjaran
- Used to express intense emotion, drama, and passion
- Very common in climactic moments and emotional peaks
