# Jins Iraq

## Overview

Jins Iraq is a 4-note jins. It is the first jins in Maqam Iraq. Its defining characteristic is the use of three consecutive neutral seconds, giving it a unique symmetrical sound that doesn't exist in any other jins in the system.

---

## Interval Structure

- **Total span**: Perfect 4th (500¢) from tonic to ghammaz
- **Pattern**: N2 - N2 - N2 (150¢ - 150¢ - 150¢)
- **Character**: Mystical, symmetrical, otherworldly

## Distinctive Features

- Three consecutive neutral seconds (150¢ each) — unique among ajnas
- The perfect symmetry of equal intervals gives it a floating, otherworldly quality
- Uses two half-flat notes (B𝄳 and D𝄳) plus another half-flat at the ghammaz (E𝄳)
- Strongly associated with Sufi music and spiritual contexts
