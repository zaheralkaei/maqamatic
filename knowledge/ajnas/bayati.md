# Jins Bayati

## Overview

Jins Bayati is a 4-note jins. It is the first jins in Maqam Bayati and one of the most important and widely used ajnas in Arabic music.

---

## Scale (on D)

```
(Baggage)        Tonic                 Ghammaz         (Baggage)
    ↓              ↓                      ↓                ↓
  (B𝄳) -- (C) -- D -------- E𝄳 ------- F -------- G -- (A) -- (B♭)
                      N2        N2         M2
                     (150¢)    (150¢)     (200¢)
```

Note: E𝄳 and B𝄳 represent E half-flat and B half-flat

## Interval Structure

- **Total span**: Perfect 4th (500¢)
- **Pattern**: N2 - N2 - M2 (150¢ - 150¢ - 200¢)
- **Character**: Tender, melancholic, deeply emotional

## Distinctive Features

- Contains the characteristic **neutral second** (3 quarter-tones / 150¢)
- Two consecutive neutral seconds give Bayati its distinctive plaintive quality
- The half-flat on the 2nd degree is essential to the Bayati character

## Usage

- **Root jins** of Maqam Bayati
- One of the most common and beloved ajnas in Arabic music
- Expresses tenderness, longing, and gentle melancholy
- Very common in love songs and emotional pieces
