# Jins Nahawand Murassa'

## Overview

Jins Nahawand Murassa' has no ghammaz, and therefore no defined size. Its melody is centered around its tonic. It is the first jins in Maqam Nahawand Murassa'.

## Interval Structure

- **No ghammaz**: Melody centers around the tonic
- **No defined size**: Due to lack of ghammaz
- **Pattern**: M2 - m2 - M2 - m2 - A2
- **Character**: Dark minor with augmented second, ornate

## Distinctive Features

- Begins like Nahawand (C D E♭ F)
- The G♭ (instead of G natural) creates a darker quality
- Contains an **augmented second** (G♭ to A), adding a Hijaz-like tension
- The name "Murassa'" means "adorned" or "embellished"

## Comparison with Jins Nahawand

| Jins | Notes | Character |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Nahawand | C D E♭ F G | Simple minor |
| Nahawand Murassa' | C D E♭ F G♭ A | Ornate, with augmented 2nd |

## Usage

- **Root jins** of Maqam Nahawand Murassa'
- Creates a more ornate, dramatic version of the Nahawand sound
- The augmented second adds exotic tension to the minor quality
