# Jins Mukhalif Sharqi

## Overview

Jins Mukhalif Sharqi has no ghammaz, and therefore no defined size. Its melody is centered around its tonic. It is not part of any maqam scale and usually occurs as a modulation from Jins Sikah.

---

      (100¢)    (100¢)

Note: E𝄳 represents E half-flat

## Interval Structure

- **No ghammaz**: Melody centers around the tonic
- **No defined size**: Due to lack of ghammaz
- **Pattern**: m2 - m2 (100¢ - 100¢)
- **Character**: Tense, compressed, unusual

## Distinctive Features

- Two consecutive minor seconds create a very compressed, tense sound
- Shares the E half-flat tonic with Jins Sikah
- The G♭ (instead of G natural in Sikah) creates the distinctive "Mukhalif" (different/contrary) quality

## Comparison with Jins Sikah

| Jins | Notes | Character |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Sikah | E𝄳 F G | Ethereal, mystical |
| Mukhalif Sharqi | E𝄳 F G♭ | Tense, compressed |

## Usage

- **Not a root jins**: Does not start any maqam
- **Modulation target**: Usually occurs as a modulation from Jins Sikah
- Creates tension and contrast when departing from the more open Sikah sound
