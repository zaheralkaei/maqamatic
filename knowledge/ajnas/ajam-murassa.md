# Jins 'Ajam Murassa'

## Overview

Jins 'Ajam Murassa' is a 5-note jins. It is not part of any maqam scale and usually occurs as a modulation from Jins 'Ajam.

---

## Interval Structure

- **Total span**: Perfect 5th (700¢)
- **Pattern**: M2 - M2 - M2 - m2
- **Character**: Major with raised 4th (Lydian quality)

## Comparison with Jins 'Ajam

| Jins | Pattern | Notes (transposed to same tonic) |
|------|---------|----------------------------------|
| 'Ajam | M2 - M2 - m2 - M2 | F G A B♭ C |
| 'Ajam Murassa' | M2 - M2 - M2 - m2 | F G A B C |

The difference: 'Ajam Murassa' has a natural 4th degree (B) instead of the lowered 4th (B♭) in regular 'Ajam, creating a Lydian-like quality.

## Usage

- **Not a root jins**: Does not start any maqam
- **Modulation target**: Usually occurs as a modulation from Jins 'Ajam
- **Character**: Brighter, more tense than regular 'Ajam due to the raised 4th
