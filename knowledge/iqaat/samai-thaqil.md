# Iqa' Sama'i Thaqil 10/8

**Sama'i Thaqil** (Samā'ī Thaqīl) — 10/8, 10 eighths per measure.

## Overview

> Iqa‘ Sama‘i Thaqil (also called Sama‘i, for short) is a very popular iqa‘ in the Muwashah vocal genre, as well as the principal iqa‘ used in the Sama‘i Turkish/Ottoman instrumental form.
>
> — [MaqamWorld](https://www.maqamworld.com/en/iqaa/samai_thaqil.php)

---

## Pattern

**Notation:** `D - T - - D - T - -`  
**Base unit:** eighth | **Total divisions:** 40 (per measure)

**Legend:** `D` = Dum (bass, accent 2) | `T` = Tak (sharp, accent 1) | `k` = Ka (ghost note, no accent) | `-` = Rest

**Events (position, duration, accent):**

```
D(0,4,2) -(4,4,0) T(8,4,1) -(12,4,0) -(16,4,0) D(20,4,2) -(24,4,0) T(28,4,1) -(32,4,0) -(36,4,0)
```

## Tempo

- **Typical:** 80 BPM
- **Range:** 60–100 BPM

## Feel

Elegant, limping.

## Usage

- **Genres:** classical, Ottoman
- **Regions:** Turkey, Levant, Egypt

## Form grammar

Main iqa' for the **Sama'i** form (10/8 Ottoman instrumental).

## MIDI

- **Dum (D):** note 36 (C2)
- **Tak (T):** note 42 (F#2)
- **Ka (k):** note 39 (D#2)
