# Iqa' Sama'i Darij 3/4

**Sama'i Darij** (Samā'ī Dārij) — 3/4, 6 eighths per measure.

## Overview

> Iqa‘ Sama‘i Darij (also called Darij, for short) is a general 3/4 iqa‘ used both in vocal and instrumental genre. Iqa‘ Sama‘i Darij is also the principal iqa‘ used in the Darij instrumental composed form.
>
> — [MaqamWorld](https://www.maqamworld.com/en/iqaa/samai_darij.php)

---

## Pattern

**Notation:** `D T T`  
**Base unit:** eighth | **Total divisions:** 24 (per measure)

**Legend:** `D` = Dum (bass, accent 2) | `T` = Tak (sharp, accent 1) | `k` = Ka (ghost note, no accent) | `-` = Rest

**Events (position, duration, accent):**

```
D(0,8,2) T(8,8,1) T(16,8,0)
```

## Tempo

- **Typical:** 130 BPM
- **Range:** 100–160 BPM

## Feel

Waltz-like, flowing.

## Usage

- **Genres:** Ottoman classical, sama'i
- **Regions:** Turkey, Levant, Egypt

## Form grammar

**Khana 4** (return section) for the Bashraf, Sama'i, and Longa forms — the traditional Ottoman practice.

## MIDI

- **Dum (D):** note 36 (C2)
- **Tak (T):** note 42 (F#2)
- **Ka (k):** note 39 (D#2)
