# Iqa' Yuruk Semai 6/8

**Yuruk Semai** (Yürük Semāī) — 6/8, 6 eighths per measure.

## Overview

> Iqa‘ Yuruk Semai is originally a Turkish usul. It is very popular in the Muwashah genre, and is alternatively called Yugrug. When it accelerates, it may modulate to Iqa‘ Sama‘i Darij in 3/4 or Iqa‘ Sama‘i Saraband in 3/8.
>
> — [MaqamWorld](https://www.maqamworld.com/en/iqaa/yuruk_semai.php)

---

## Pattern

**Notation:** `D T T D T -`  
**Base unit:** eighth | **Total divisions:** 24 (per measure)

**Legend:** `D` = Dum (bass, accent 2) | `T` = Tak (sharp, accent 1) | `k` = Ka (ghost note, no accent) | `-` = Rest

**Events (position, duration, accent):**

```
D(0,4,2) T(4,4,1) T(8,4,0) D(12,4,2) T(16,4,1) -(20,4,0)
```

## Tempo

- **Typical:** 130 BPM
- **Range:** 100–160 BPM

## Feel

Quick, dance-like.

## Usage

- **Genres:** Turkish classical, sama'i
- **Regions:** Turkey, Levant

## MIDI

- **Dum (D):** note 36 (C2)
- **Tak (T):** note 42 (F#2)
- **Ka (k):** note 39 (D#2)
